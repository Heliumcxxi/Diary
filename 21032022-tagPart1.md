Typesetting and Tags

Common structural tags:

<h1> <p> <hr> <br> <div> <span> <pre>

<h1>Heading tags:

    <h1> is the largest while <h6> is the smallest heading tags.

    Common used attribute - align: left, center and right

<p> paragraph tag

    For paragraph identification. Only add text, pic and chart inside. No <a> or <input>

<hr> Horizontal tag

    Separating web page, paragraph by paragraph

    <noshade> flat line effect

<br> Force new line

Difference between <div> & <span>

    <div>
        - Default no effect.
        - Will occupy a full row. 
        - Can put any tag inside.
    <span> 
        - Multiple <span> can exit in the same row. 
        - Attribute restrction similar to <p>. 

<pre>

    Retain all spaces inside the tag
